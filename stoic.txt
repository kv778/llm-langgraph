Stoic Quotes from Marcus Aurelius:

1. "The soul becomes dyed with the color of its thoughts." — Marcus Aurelius
2. "You have power over your mind – not outside events. Realize this, and you will be immune to despair, fear, and even life itself." — Marcus Aurelius
3. "The way to get started is to quit talking and begin doing." — Marcus Aurelius
4. "It is not that we have a short time to live, but that we constantly lose time." — Marcus Aurelius
5. "The happiness of your life depends upon the quality of your thoughts: therefore, guard your thoughts." — Marcus Aurelius
